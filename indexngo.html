Together We Can Save Lives

Codebeny Foundation (C.B.F) is a non-profit organization founded by Aidoo Benjamin, which aims to provide access to quality education and digital skills to underserved communities, particularly in Africa.

About Us

Codebeny Foundation focuses on empowering individuals, especially youth and women, with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the digital economy. Through its programs and initiatives, Codebeny Foundation provides training, mentorship, and resources in areas such as coding, software development, and technology entrepreneurship, ultimately seeking to bridge the digital divide and promote economic inclusion and social impact. By fostering a community of innovators and change-makers, Codebeny Foundation strives to create a brighter future for individuals, communities, and societies across Africa.
